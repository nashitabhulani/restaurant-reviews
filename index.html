Some of the best-rated restaurants

FOOD HOUSE

All of our menu items are inspired by continental cuisine and have been created by our head chef, after studying authentic continenta cuisine in Paris. Not only do we have fresh flown-in seafood from the northeast, but we also have a variety of handcrafted cocktails, wine, and beer to choose from. Come dine with us & experience an authentic continental meal in an intimate dining space. We look forward to serving you!

Luke's Diner

At Luke's, we’re serving up more than breakfast. In fact, Luke's Famous burgers is one of our unexpected specialties. Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than four decades, is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it’s topped with Lukes signature herb-garlic butter, then carved tableside.

The Core

Core specializes in delicious food featuring fresh ingredients and masterful preparation by the Core culinary team. Whether you’re ordering a multi-course meal or grabbing a drink and pizza at the bar, Core lively, casual yet upscale atmosphere makes it perfect for dining with friends, family, clients and business associates.

Meraki

Meraki combines flavors & inspiration from the Far East & the West to create what we think is the best! Home to the original penne-pasta, we specialize in pizzas, as well as handmade appetizers, sandwiches, salads, and gluten friendly, vegetarian, & vegan selections. Feel free to indulge in a tiki drink or craft beer with our fantastic unique cuisine. Cheers!
